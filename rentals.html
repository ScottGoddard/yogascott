Rentals

Chart your own course! Explore the waters of Key West at your own pace with a boat rental from Fury. Whether you want to snorkel the reef, spend the day sunning and splashing on a natural sand bar or enjoy a picnic on one of our remote islands, you can make your own way or enjoy a guided trip with one of our friendly and experienced captains.

We provide a boats, rods and full gear for the day. Arrive early so we can provide the equipment you need. We are located in Clearwater Florida. After choosing your rental boat here in Clearwater you can also rent tackle for the big fish and when you are on your way out to sea check out our fish database to see what is in season and hitting this time of year.
